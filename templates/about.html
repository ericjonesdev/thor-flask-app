About Us

Hi there! I am the creator of Foretell & Company, an Afrofuturism website that aims to explore the intersection of autochthonous culture and science fiction. I've always been fascinated with the idea of a future that is inclusive and representative of all people, especially those of aboriginal descent. That's why I started this platform, to imagine and showcase a world where autochthonous people not only exist, but thrive and shape the future on their own terms. Foretell & Company features articles, podcasts, and videos that delve into the realm of Afrofuturism and its impact on art, music, film, technology, and more. I want to provide a space for autochthonous creatives to showcase their works, share their thoughts and ideas, and engage in conversations about the future of our communities. I'm passionate about this project and I hope to inspire others to join me in this journey of exploring the vast potential of Afrofuturism. Join me and let's imagine a future that's not only diverse, but bold and unapologetically powerful!

Eric Jones
